About Me

As I mentioned above, some About Me pages are two sentences and some feel like an endless scroll of TMI. Your About Me page needs to be short enough to retain people’s attention so that they read through it all but not so short that they can’t get any sense of who you are. Anything longer than half a Word Doc page is too long. Save the really lengthy and in-depth about me stories for a blog post.
